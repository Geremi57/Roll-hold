How to play

Welcome to the Random Two-Player Dice 🎲 Game! 🎯

Objective: Be the first to score 100 points to win the game!

🌀 How It Works: Roll the dice 🎲 to add the number to your current score.

You can keep rolling to accumulate points, but...

⚠️ Watch Out! If you roll a 1, you lose your entire current score for that round. 😱

Feeling lucky? Roll again!

Feeling cautious? Press Hold to secure your current score.

💡 Tips: Play it safe if you sense a 1 might be coming.

Don’t get overly ambitious—remember, it’s just a game! 😄

Good luck and have fun! 🥳
